What is JMU Outdoors Club?


The Outdoor Adventure Club is a club for people of all skill and fitness levels. We try to plan trips as often as possible (hiking, camping, skiing, backpacking, etc).


If you have questions, you can join our Groupme chat with this link (https://groupme.com/join_group/69233791/ni2uIdiL) or contact one of us!
